About the Book

Wings of God is an autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India. It captures his early life, struggles, and his remarkable journey through India's defense and space programs.

The book serves as a source of inspiration to young Indians and is a testament to dedication, humility, and the power of dreams.
